What Is a Headpat or Petpet GIF?

A headpat GIF is a short looping animation in which a hand gently pats a person, pet, avatar, or fictional character. The subject compresses and rebounds with each movement, which gives the GIF its recognizable soft and playful look. You may also see the format called a head pat meme, petting GIF, petpet GIF, or simply a petpet.

These names usually refer to the same visual effect. The wording changes between communities, but the search intent is consistent: upload an image, line it up beneath the hand, preview the movement, and save a small animated reaction. The format is also associated with the broader Pet the X emote style, where almost any character or object can become the subject of the pat.

Tips for a Better Petpet GIF

  • Start with a clear subject. A face or character with strong contrast is easier to read at 112 x 112 pixels.
  • Leave room above the head. A little empty space gives the hand somewhere to move without covering the entire face.
  • Use positioning mode. Drag with a mouse or touch, or use the arrow keys, until the pat lands on the main focal point.
  • Tune squish with restraint. More squish creates a stronger meme effect, while a lower value keeps photos easier to recognize.
  • Check the animation speed. A readable loop usually works better than a very fast one, especially for Discord reactions and emotes.
  • Try mirror mode. Flipping the subject can improve the composition when a face or character naturally leans to one side.
